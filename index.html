RUET GIS CLUB

Welcome to RUET GIS CLUB, an exclusive community for undergraduate students at RUET passionate about Geographic Information Systems (GIS). Here, we harness geospatial technology to tackle real-world challenges and create meaningful impact. Whether you're new to GIS or seasoned, join us to learn, collaborate, and innovate. Dive into workshops, events, and resources tailored to undergraduate students. Connect with like-minded peers and engage in impactful projects within our university community. If you're an RUET undergraduate with a passion for GIS, join us in shaping the future together.
